## 4. Rearranging Formulas

Given the pendulum formula

$$
T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}
$$

Solve for $g$.

First divide by $2\pi$:

$$
\frac{T}{2\pi} = \sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}
$$

Square both sides:

$$
\left(\frac{T}{2\pi}\right)^2 = \frac{L}{g}
$$

Rearrange for $g$:

$$
g = \frac{L}{\left(\frac{T}{2\pi}\right)^2}
$$

Simplify:

$$
g = \frac{4\pi^2 L}{T^2}
$$

### Final answer

$$
g = \frac{4\pi^2 L}{T^2}
$$
