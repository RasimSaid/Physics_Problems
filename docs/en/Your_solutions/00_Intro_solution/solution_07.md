## 7. Logic & Series

A bicycle is 10 m from a wall and moves toward it at 1 m/s.

So the bicycle reaches the wall in

$$
t = \frac{10}{1} = 10 \text{ s}
$$

The fly keeps moving continuously at 2 m/s until the bicycle reaches the wall and crushes it.

Therefore, total distance traveled by the fly is

$$
d = vt = 2 \cdot 10 = 20 \text{ m}
$$

### Final answer

$$
20 \text{ m}
$$
