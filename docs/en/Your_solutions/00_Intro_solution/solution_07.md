## 7. Logic & Series

A bicycle is **10 m** away from a wall and moves toward it with a speed of **1 m/s**.

### Situation Diagram

```text
Wall                        Bicycle
 |                            🚲
 |<--------- 10 m ----------->|
```

The time it takes for the bicycle to reach the wall is

$$
t = \frac{d}{v}
$$

Substituting the values:

$$
t = \frac{10}{1} = 10 \text{ s}
$$

So the bicycle reaches the wall in **10 seconds**.

---

### Motion of the Fly

The fly moves continuously at **2 m/s** between the bicycle and the wall.

```text
Wall           🪰↔↔↔↔↔↔↔↔↔           Bicycle
 |-----------------------------------🚲
```

The fly keeps flying **until the bicycle reaches the wall**.

Instead of calculating each individual trip, we observe that the fly simply flies for the **entire duration of the motion**, which is **10 seconds**.

---

### Distance Traveled by the Fly

Using the distance formula

$$
d = vt
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
d = 2 \cdot 10 = 20 \text{ m}
$$

---

### Final Answer

$$
20 \text{ m}
$$
