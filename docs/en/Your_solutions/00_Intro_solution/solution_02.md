## 2. Systems of Equations

Solve the system:

$$
2x + 3y = 12
$$

$$
x - y = 1
$$

From the second equation:

$$
x = y + 1
$$

Substitute into the first equation:

$$
2(y+1) + 3y = 12
$$

$$
2y + 2 + 3y = 12
$$

$$
5y = 10
$$

$$
y = 2
$$

Then,

$$
x = y + 1 = 3
$$

### Final answer

$$
x = 3,\quad y = 2
$$
