## 3. Proportionality

Universal Law of Gravitation:

$F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}$

Changes:

$m_1 \rightarrow \frac{m_1}{2}$  
$m_2 \rightarrow \frac{m_2}{2}$  
$r \rightarrow 2r$

New force:

$F' = G \frac{(m_1/2)(m_2/2)}{(2r)^2}$

$F' = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{16r^2}$

$F' = \frac{F}{16}$

**The gravitational force becomes 1/16 of the original.**
