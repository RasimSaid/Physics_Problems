## 5. Relative Velocity

River velocity $\vec{v}_r = 2\hat{i}$, Boat speed relative to water $v_b = 5 \text{ m/s}$.

* **Direction:** To go directly North, the horizontal component of the boat's velocity must cancel the river's flow:
    $$5 \cdot \sin\theta = 2 \implies \theta = \arcsin(0.4) \approx 23.58^\circ \text{ (West of North)}$$
* **Time:** Vertical speed $v_y = 5 \cdot \cos(23.58^\circ) \approx 4.58 \text{ m/s}$.
    $$t = \frac{200 \text{ m}}{4.58 \text{ m/s}} \approx 43.6 \text{ s}$$
