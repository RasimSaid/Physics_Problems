## 7. Elimination of Time

$x = 2t^2, y = 3t^3$

* **Eliminate $t$:** $t = \sqrt{x/2} \implies y = 3\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^{3/2}$ or $y^2 = \frac{27}{8}x^3$.
* **Calculations:**
    * $\vec{v}(t) = (4t, 9t^2)$
    * $|\vec{v}(t)| = \sqrt{16t^2 + 81t^4}$
    * $\vec{a}(t) = (4, 18t)$
    * $|\vec{a}(t)| = \sqrt{16 + 324t^2}$
* **Is acceleration constant?** No, it depends on $t$.
