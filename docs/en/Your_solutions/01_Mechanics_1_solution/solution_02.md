## 2. Range Optimization

To find the angle $\theta$ that maximizes $R(\theta) = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}$, we take the derivative with respect to $\theta$ and set it to zero:
$$\frac{dR}{d\theta} = \frac{v_0^2}{g} \cdot \cos(2\theta) \cdot 2 = 0$$
$$\cos(2\theta) = 0 \implies 2\theta = 90^\circ \implies \theta = 45^\circ$$
Thus, the maximum range is achieved at **45°**.
