# Section 1: Mechanics I - Solutions

## 1. Projectile Motion

**Given:** $v_0 = 100 \text{ m/s}$, $\theta = 37^\circ$, $g \approx 9.8 \text{ m/s}^2$
($\sin 37^\circ \approx 0.6$, $\cos 37^\circ \approx 0.8$)

* **Differential Equations:**
    Since air resistance is neglected, the only acceleration is gravity acting downwards:
    $$\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} = 0, \quad \frac{d^2y}{dt^2} = -g$$

* **Time of Flight ($t_{f}$):**
    The projectile returns to ground when $y(t) = 0$.
    $$t_{f} = \frac{2v_0 \sin\theta}{g} = \frac{2 \cdot 100 \cdot 0.6}{9.8} \approx 12.24 \text{ s}$$

* **Maximum Height ($H_{max}$):**
    Occurs when vertical velocity $v_y = 0$.
    $$H_{max} = \frac{(v_0 \sin\theta)^2}{2g} = \frac{60^2}{19.6} \approx 183.67 \text{ m}$$

* **Range ($R$):**
    $$R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g} = \frac{100^2 \cdot \sin(74^\circ)}{9.8} \approx 980.8 \text{ m}$$
